Ginatanang Bilo Bilo Recipe

(1. Bilo-Bilo) cup of glutinous rice flour, ½ cup water, ½ Tsp ube extract. (2. Sago) 1 cup sago (qater for boiling Ginataan), 1 can Coconut milk, 1-2 cups water for diluting as needed, 1 ½ sweet potato cubed and peeled, 1 plantain or banana sliced, 1 cup sweetened

In a mixng bowl, combine rice and flour then add water slowly in order to get that sticky texture. Once its done, use your hands and shape them into balls. Along the process, add one half of ube and mix it thoroughly. Set the bilo balls for later. Use a pot and add water and coconut milk to low boil then put Sago inside and boil for about 20 minutes. then add the sweet potatoes in around 5-10 mins. lower the heat then add the Bilo-bilo and let it simmer for 3 mins. then add the jackfruit, sago and banana for 1-2mins.
