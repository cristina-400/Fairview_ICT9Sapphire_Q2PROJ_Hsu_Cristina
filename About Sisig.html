Sisig Recipe

1 lb pig ears, 1. lb. pig snout, 1lb pork belly, 2 pieces onions minced, 3 pieces bay leaves, 2 teapoons salt, 4 thumbs ginger crushed, 1 quart water. Now for the Sisig dressing: ½ cup Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise, 2 tablespoons sukang iloko, ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon sugar, ¼ cup liver spread , 2 limes, 1 tablespoon Knorr Liquid Seasoning. For Equipment, use 1 Grill or either use gas or charcoal grill.

First we have to boil our pig ears, pork belly, bay leaves 2, teaspoons of salt, 1 quart water and a cooking pot. Put this ingriedents inside and wait for 1 hour. When its done, take out the pot and drain your water. Start heating up the grill and put the placed the meat we prepared earlier. Cook them for 5 minutes and filp them over. Seperate the cooked pig parts and place it in a container to let it cool down. Now, we chopped the pig parts. Combine the dressing components and the pig parts together in a bowl. Its optional to put fried egg in your sisig.
