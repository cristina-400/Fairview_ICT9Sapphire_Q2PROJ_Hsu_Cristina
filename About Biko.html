2 cups glutinous rice aka sticky rice or malagkit. 1 ½ cups water, 2 cups brown sugar, 4 cups coconut milk, ½ tsp salt.

Combine the sticky rice and water into the rice cooker (combine lesser amount of water so that the rice will not be fully cooked) during that, get a pot and put it in low heat. then put brown sugar and salt and stir it until the texture becomes thick. Once the rice is cooked, put the rice together with the thick texture and put cocnut milk. stir until all the ingriedents evaporates. and now, biko is ready to serve!
