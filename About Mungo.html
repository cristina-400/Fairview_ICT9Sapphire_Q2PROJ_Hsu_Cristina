Munggo Recipe

1 1/2 cups Mung beans. 1 tbsp garlic, ½ lb pork thinly sliced, 2 cups spinach (or alugbati), 1 piece tomato chopped, 1 piece onion chopped, 8 pieces shrimp optional, 2 tablespoons fish sauce, 24 ounces water for boiling, 1 piece Knorr beef cube for flavoring, 1/2 cup crushed pork rind chicharon, 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Firstly we gather 24 ounces water. Pour it into the pot along with the munggo beans. Boil for 35-50 minutes. while waiting use 1 tablespoon of garlic, and medium-sized tomato, both should be chopped. put these into a seperate pan and start sautéing together. then ½ add slice of pork and let the ingriedents cook nicely for 5 minutes. you can also put water to ensure that the pork is fully cook. afterwards, put 1 beef cube and 2 teaspoon of fish sauce for flavoring. Let this shimmer for 10 minutes. Now, add 8 pieces of shrimp inside (This can be optional) to add the saltiness of your Munggo! Stir all the ingriedents together and cook it for 5 minutes. Let's grab the munggo we left from earlier, add them to the pan and stir. Let the ingriedents slimmer for 10 minutes. then, add 2 cups of spinach and ½ cup of crushed pork or chicharon inside. and for our final ingriedent, add a touch of black pepper.
